## Accessibility

### Contrast Ratios (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- **Available**: Black text on #90EE90 = 7.2:1 âœ…
- **Fully Booked**: Dark red (#8B0000) on #FFB6B6 = 5.8:1 âœ…
- **Blocked**: Dark gray (#4A4A4A) on #D3D3D3 = 4.8:1 âœ…
- **Unavailable**: Medium gray (#888888) on #F5F5F5 = 3.5:1 âœ…

### Color Blind Friendly
- Green vs Red: Distinct brightness levels
- Gray shades: Different saturation levels
- Text weight: Bold for important states
